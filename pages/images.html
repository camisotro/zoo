Cycling in Portland

Portland's mild West Coast weather is a perfect fit for a city with over 300 miles of bicycle routes, including bike lanes, greenways and off-street paths. The MAX rail system has convenient bicycle racks for multimodal trips. Zoobomb riders meet downtown at the Pile and catch the MAX.

Portland's downtown terrain is mostly flat, but the city's west side borders on the Tualatin Mountains. The Oregon Zoo is located in Washington Park which sits at an elevation of up to 870 feet above sea level. The whole Zoobomb experience is powered by gravity, and the elevator you see here makes that possible.
